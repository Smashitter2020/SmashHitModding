Installing Mods Warning

  • This only works if you install the first mod app and the second mod app allowed multiple mod apps to be installed. This might lead to an overwrite or an installation error.
  • If APKs are modified, it must be resigned in order to allow this APK file to be installed. Without resigning it, the mod app may prevent installing it due to an error.
  • It's important to have mods compatible on your device. When you open the Smash Hit mod app, the dialog will appear on an older version or non-compatible on your device.
  • Be careful when installing Smash Hit mod apps on your device, Google Play Protect may block apps that contain unsafe or harmful. Click on the Install anyway button, otherwise it will prevent installing it.
