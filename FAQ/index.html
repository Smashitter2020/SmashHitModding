Here are some frequency asked questions about Smash Hit mods.

What is it?

Smash Hit mods are creative and technical playground where users can create mods by Smash Hit, such as modifying game assets and segments together. You can make mods by using Blender or Shatter Cilent.

How do I download Smash Hit mods on my device?

You can download Smash Hit mods on your device, including one of the websites that you can use: Smash Hit Lab, Google Drive, and Shards Community.

If you still unable to download Smash Hit mod apps on your device, and have no solutions to work, you can ask the owners to provide about your mod.

Why is it important to use Smash Hit mods with caution?

While Smash Hit mods unlock exciting custom features and gameplay enhancements, using them without care can lead to serious issues. Mods often involve altering the game’s original files, which can cause crashes or glitches if assets are improperly modified, unstable, containing game bugs, break compatibility with future game updates or official content, risk data loss if you don’t back up your original files beforehand, or it may violate terms of service such as unlocking achievements too quickly.

How do I create a custom checkpoint for Smash Hit mods?

Custom checkpoints and levels are built from modding, which can exist from Blender and Shatter OSS, from JSON or XML scripts. There is no limit to the number of checkpoints you can make, but having too many checkpoints may increase the Smash Hit mod app's size.

How do I create Smash Hit mods?

To create mods for Smash Hit, you need a few requirements including: APK Editor Studio, Notepad++, Blender and Shatter OSS, and an Android/Samsung device.

Make sure to meet the mininum system requirements. If you don't have APK Studio, Notepad++, and Blender on your computer, you may need to download it from the website, otherwise it will not work properly.

When installing the Smash Hit mod, uninstall the original Smash Hit game (can lead to conflicts) in order to back up your data first, then install the modded APK on your Android device.

What are the main game functions for Smash Hit mods?

Main Game (mg) Functions are a type of scripting for Smash Hit mods can only include Lua or XML, including functions like mgFogColor, mgSegment, and mgRotate. For example, mgFogColor sets the color of a gradient, mgSegment is used to place segments in a room or a checkpoint, but returns the segment length, and mgRotate is a command used to rotate stages and checkpoints. Sometimes main game functions can include commands.
